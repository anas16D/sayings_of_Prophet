Year(A.D) Event
570 Birth of the Prophet Muhammad (the Prophet's father died a few months before his birth)
The Holy Prophet (S.A.W) was born in Makkah, on 12 Rabi-ul-Awal, 570 A.D. He belonged to the Banu Hashim clan of the great tribe Quraish. His father was Abdullah bin Abdul Muttalib and his mother was Aminah. Before the birth of the Holy Prophet (S.A.W), his father died in a commercial visit to Syria. Four months after the death of Abdullah, Mohammad (S.A.W)’s mother gave birth to him. He was named Mohammad (S.A.W) by his grandfather, Abdul Muttalib while Aminah named him Ahmed.
576 Death of the Prophet's mother Aminah, when the Prophet is sixy ear old
578 Death of the Prophet's grandfather, Abdul Muttalib
595 The Prophet's marriage to Khadijah The Holy Prophet (S.A.W) was famous for his truthfulness and honesty for which he was given the titles of ‘Al Sadiq’and ‘Al Amin’ by the Makkans. Hazrat Khadija too was aware of these marvellous attributes of the Prophet. On the requirement of a trustworthy man to lead her caravan, Hazrat Khadija (R.A) called him; his hiring for that purpose became finalized. On this journey to Syria, Hazrat Khadija (R.A)’s slave Maysarah accompanied the Prophet (S.A.W). Prophet (S.A.W) worked diligently during the trip and earned a great deal of profit. When they came back, Khadija (R.A) was told about Prophet (S.A.W)’s honesty by her slave Maysarah who has greatly praised Mohammad (S.A.W)’s virtues and trading skills. Now Hazrat Khadija (R.A) was so impressed that she sent a marriage proposal through her friend Nafeesah to him, which he (S.A.W) approved. At the time of marriage, Hazrat Khadija (R.A) was 40 years old while Mohammad (S.A.W) was 25 years old. The marriage was successful. They were blessed with four daughters and two sons; Ruqayyah, Zaynab, Umme Kulthoom, Fatima, Abdullah and Ibrahim (both sons were passed away during childhood).
